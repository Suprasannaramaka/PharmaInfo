Overview

Drug Composition: Paracetamol 500mg

Category: Over-the-counter (OTC)

Usage

Indications: Fever, mild to moderate pain.

Dosage: 1 tablet every 4-6 hours. Do not exceed 4g per day.

Precautions

Not suitable for individuals with liver disorders.

Avoid alcohol consumption while taking this medicine.

Side Effects

Common: Nausea, dizziness.

Severe: Allergic reactions, liver damage (in case of overdose).

Storage

Store at room temperature (15-25°C). Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight.
